I'm very thankful to the [Visual Evidence Lab](https://www.colorado.edu/lab/visualevidence/), [Sandra Ristovska](https://www.colorado.edu/cmci/people/media-studies/sandra-ristovska), and Katie Considine for including me in the third episode of [Seeking Justice with Video and AI](https://open.spotify.com/show/5KVqFWaGSCUjEh2VaSqTiI).

With generative AI blurring the line between real and fake, we unpack how deepfakes spread, how images get weaponised, and the solutions emerging to help verify genuine footage. I'm honoured to share the conversation with [Sam Gregory](https://www.linkedin.com/in/samgregory/), executive director of [WITNESS](https://www.witness.org/), whose work on digital authenticity and provenance has been pioneering.
