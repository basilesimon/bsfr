I'm looking forward to returning to Perugia for the [International Journalism Festival](https://www.journalismfestival.com/) and the stimulating discussions and encounters it brings about every year.

This year I'll be part of a panel on **"Witness to War: How Journalists Can Safeguard Digital Evidence for Justice"**, alongside [Emily Tripp](https://www.linkedin.com/in/emily-tripp-8a79b61a4/) of the [Starling Lab](https://www.starlinglab.org/), [Frederik Obermaier](https://www.linkedin.com/in/frederik-obermaier/) of [paper trail media](https://www.papertrailmedia.de/), and [Rebecca Bakos Blumenthal](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rebecca-bakos-blumenthal/).

Our conversation will offer actionable guidance for newsrooms working in conflict zones and for journalists aiming to ensure their documentation can support future accountability efforts.

The [full programme is now available](https://www.journalismfestival.com/programme).
